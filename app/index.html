What is Mavic 2 Pro?

The Mavic 2 Pro drone is an engineering marvel, perfect for aerial photography. The drone incorporates all of DJI's best technologies, transforming the world of aerial photography.

The Mavic 2 Pro is equipped with the all-new Hasselblad L1D-20c camera. The L1D-20c camera operates with the unique Hasselblad Natural Colour Solution (HNCS) technology, enabling users to capture magnificent aerial photos with 20-megapixel resolution and stunning colors.

Have Questions?

What are the differences between Mavic 2 Pro and Mavic 2 Zoom?

Mavic 2 Pro and Mavic 2 Zoom differ mainly in their camera capabilities. The Mavic 2 Pro features a Hasselblad camera with a 1-inch CMOS sensor and supports Dlog-M 10-bit color profile, while the Mavic 2 Zoom offers a zoom lens that enables dynamic perspective shots.

How is Mavic 2 better than Mavic Pro?

Compared to the Mavic Pro, the Mavic 2 offers significant improvements in camera quality, flight time, obstacle avoidance, and intelligent flight modes. The Mavic 2 also introduces new features like Hyperlapse, enhancing the overall aerial photography experience.

Can Mavic 2 be connected to DJI Goggles?

Yes, the Mavic 2 can be connected to DJI Goggles, providing an immersive first-person view (FPV) flying experience. This integration allows users to enjoy real-time video transmission in high definition while piloting the drone.

Is Mavic 2 waterproof?

No, the Mavic 2 is not waterproof. While it is designed to withstand light rain and moisture, it is not suitable for flying in heavy rain or water submersion. It's essential to exercise caution and avoid wet conditions to prevent damage to the drone.
